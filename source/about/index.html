About

Simply referred to as Chavez. I came up through Ruby on Rails and iOS consulting into the tech and startup world in Chandler, AZ with Integrum. I'm well versed in Agile, Scrum and XP as these have been the tools I have used to learn about each project I have been a part of.

I moved on from consulting to help build the foundations of AuthorityLabs. Growing in the ways of big data and scaling was what happened here daily. Approaching problems with this in mind helped me learn about myself as a programmer and engineer and came to love the term of defensive coding.

Still wanting to learn more and cut my chops more on the engineering side I made the move out to San Francisco. I found a home with Kiip, pronounced keep. I further expanded my toolset jumping into Python. Here I learned Go, a modern language from Google, and implemented several services to optimize the Python stack to help scale the Kiip API even further.

I try to spend my free time exploring San Francisco, playing music, going out to see shows and continue learning. You can see some of my work on my github account. There is where I have open sourced iOS applications as well as played around with machine learning and various algoritms in languages such as Go, Python, Ruby, Julia, Objective-C, Erlang and others.
